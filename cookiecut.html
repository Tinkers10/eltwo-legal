CookieCut Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

CookieCut is local-first and built to reduce tracking.

What CookieCut stores locally

CookieCut stores your license state, license key, preferences, verification timestamps, and local banner count in Chrome storage on your device.

What leaves your device

When you activate or re-check a license, CookieCut sends the license key to Gumroad’s license verification API. Gumroad handles checkout and payment details externally.

Website access

CookieCut inspects visible button labels and related attributes on webpages only to identify cookie consent controls. It does not sell browsing data, collect analytics, or transmit page content to el-two LLC.

Remote code

CookieCut does not load remote executable code. All extension JavaScript and CSS are bundled in the Chrome extension package.
