SessionSync Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

SessionSync Pro is a local-first Chrome extension for saving and switching website sessions.

Data stored locally

SessionSync Pro stores the following in Chrome local storage on your device: account names and grouping labels you create; website cookies and session data needed for account switching; extension settings and preferences; and your Gumroad license key with limited entitlement metadata.

Data sent to Gumroad

When you activate or re-check a license, the extension sends a license verification request to Gumroad containing the Gumroad product id, the Gumroad license key, and increment_uses_count=false. Saved account names, cookies, website session data, browsing history, and extension settings are not sent to Gumroad.

No remote code

SessionSync Pro does not load remote scripts, remote SDKs, external widgets, or remotely hosted executable code.

Data deletion

You can delete local extension data with “Clear All Data” in Options or by uninstalling the extension.
